# Lean Canvas - Creed

## One-Page Business Model

---

## 1. Problem

**Top 3 Problems:**

1. **AI Search Invisibility**
   - Websites disappearing from ChatGPT, Bing Chat, Perplexity answers
   - No way to know if/how AI mentions your brand

2. **Knowledge Gap**
   - Marketers don't know how to optimize for AI
   - "Nobody knows how to do it at this point" (Reddit r/SEO)

3. **Traffic Decline**
   - Traditional search traffic dropping 25%+
   - AI providing zero-click answers
   - Publishers losing ad revenue

**Existing Alternatives:**
- DIY trial-and-error (slow, unreliable)
- Expensive enterprise tools (Profound: $$$)
- Traditional SEO tools (don't address AI)

---

## 2. Solution

**Top 3 Features:**

1. **AI Visibility Health-Check**
   - Audit showing exactly where you appear/vanish in AI
   - 0-100 score + actionable recommendations
   - 10-day turnaround

2. **Schema Generator**
   - 5 schema types (Product, Article, FAQ, How-to, Org)
   - Copy-paste implementation
   - Instant AI-friendly markup

3. **24/7 AI Mention Alerts**
   - Monitor ChatGPT, Bing, Perplexity daily
   - Instant Slack/email notifications
   - Monthly trend reports
